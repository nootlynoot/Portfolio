# 2021 Team Boon Hiatus

Development for this project has been ceased until further notice.

## Name
Ying & Yang

## Description
Yin & Yang is an immersive, 3D offline PC tower defense game designed to challenge players with strategic decision-making through the concept of balance. Players take on the role of a monk tasked with protecting the sacred Wells of Yin and Yang from waves of invading spirits seeking to disrupt the harmony between light and darkness. Through engaging, strategy-driven gameplay, players carefully place and manage unique defensive turrets, switching their alignment between Yin and Yang to counter different spirit types, and maintain the delicate balance of the well as increasingly challenging enemy waves emerge. Yin & Yang delivers a distinctive tower defense experience by combining tactical resource management, real-time adaptation, and a stylized oriental world inspired by traditional Japanese gardens and manga aesthetics.

Simply download the game using the google drive link and enjoy!

### **Objective:**
- You will play as a monk tasked to protect the sacred well
- Place turrets, swap alignments, and balance the well by letting spirits in
- Achieve a 3 star clear with a perfectly balanced well when the level ends
