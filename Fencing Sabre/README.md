## Name
Fencing: Sabre

## Description
Fencing Sabre is a 3D offline multiplayer PC game designed to introduce players to the fundamentals and excitement of sabre fencing. Players are put into the shoes of skilled sabre fencers competing in intense one-on-one duels across unique arenas inspired by both modern Olympic venues and ancient ruins. Through strategic, skill-based gameplay, players master authentic fencing mechanics such as attack priority, directional strikes, parries, stamina management, and customizable combat styles, where every decision and action influences the flow of the match. Fencing Sabre provides players with an engaging and competitive experience while offering an accessible introduction to the rules, techniques, and strategic depth of real-world sabre fencing.
